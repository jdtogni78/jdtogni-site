The integrated platform

FamilyFund is one half of a two-app platform. DS Trader — a live algorithmic trading engine — pushes positions and prices in via REST. A research/thesis layer (LLM-maintained thesis per holding, tied to SEC filings) sits on this data and is visible to both apps. The AI framing is research/thesis-management, not stock-picking.

Reliability & operations

  • 1,791 tests as a quality gate.
  • Scheduled jobs + cron in production.
  • Multi-tier backups.
  • Real, continuous family usage as the ultimate uptime test.

Roadmap

  • Goal-coaching agent — consumer-facing AI over goals and transactions (a different dataset from the trading thesis agent).
  • Document layer / receipt OCR — no file-storage layer today; a known future direction.
