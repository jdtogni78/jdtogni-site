A live algorithmic trading system with an agentic research layer.

Runs in Docker against the Interactive Brokers API, manages real accounts, and maintains an LLM-written investment thesis for every position — each tied to SEC filings, with automated drift detection.

Read the research agent GitHub See FamilyFund

Framing: this is a research and thesis-management system built on a trading engine — not an "AI stock picker." No returns or performance claims. The signal here is engineering and eval rigor.

What it does

Trades automatically across real accounts. Connects to Interactive Brokers TWS and runs rule-based rebalancing strategies against live and paper accounts, every day.
Explains every holding. A research agent reads SEC filings and maintains a written thesis per position — there's always a documented why I own this.
Tells you when the story changes. A drift detector re-reads new filings against the stored thesis and flags material deltas in the daily report.

The research agent

The part that matters most — concrete, with an eval set.

Problem
A portfolio of positions with zero documented rationale. Most retail systems pick; few explain and revisit.
Thesis store
position_theses, position_thesis_history (versioned), thesis_evidence (assertions linked to specific filing chunks). Schema lives in FamilyFund.
Thesis generator
Agent with tool-use over SEC EDGAR (free): filing search, section extraction, fundamentals lookup — produces the initial thesis per holding.
Drift detector
Scheduled job; re-reads new filings vs. the stored thesis and surfaces deltas into the daily HTML email report.
Eval harness
~50 labeled filings with precision/recall reported openly. This is what makes it a portfolio piece and not a demo.

How it works

Engine
Java 21, Maven, runs in Docker; daily cron.
Broker
Interactive Brokers TWS API; live + paper across multiple real accounts.
Data in
fin-export (Python) aggregates Monarch / Fidelity / Schwab / TechCU; SEC EDGAR for filings.
Data out
Pushes positions & prices to FamilyFund via REST (POST /api/asset_prices_bulk_update, POST /api/portfolio_assets_bulk_update).
Reporting
HTML email reports with JFreeChart charts.

Strategies

Mechanical and rule-driven — the AI layer is research, not signal generation.
